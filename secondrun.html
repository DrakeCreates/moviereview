My "The Force Awakens" Review

My Thoughts

Personally, I have never been a very big Star Wars fan, but I did enjoy episodes I-III more than many probably did. For the most part most people that I know that have gone to see the film very much enjoyed it. A few said it was ok, but overwhelmingly there is a concensus that it is a good film and worth the price of admission.
